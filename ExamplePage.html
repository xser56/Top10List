Welcom To My Top 10 Video Games of All Time!


Ever since I was a child, playing video games was something I always had a passion for.From Shooters to Story RPGS, strategy games to rythem button pressers I play it all. Here are my Top 10 Video Games of All Time!



#10 Call of Duty

Black Ops 2


At the number 10 spot is Call of Duty Black Ops 2. Out of every fps game, black ops 2 is bar far my favorite. This was my go to game back in middle school and early highschool. Getting on after a hard days of doing nothing in my classes was the best feeling ever. From its zombies mode, to its innovated campaign it had it all. The main gamemode was the multiplayer. Hopping on and dogging on kids with friends was always a great time. Unlocking all the guns, camos, prestigeing the levels, Black Ops 2 had so much to do. The different game modes as well where great, always something to do. Back Ops 2 had everything a FPS needed to be succsessful. The reason why this game is at the very end of my top 10 list is because I feel like, the other games have left a bigger impact on my gaming expience.




#9 Mass Effect 3


Mass Effect 3 is a 2012 action role-playing video game developed by BioWare and published by Electronic Arts. The third major entry in the Mass Effect series and the final installment of the original trilogy. You have to fight off an invading alien race from wiping out the universe. I placed this game at #9 because at the time, this was my first ever narritive driven RPG game that I played. The best part about this game are the characters. You learn to love them and their company throughout the story and can even develop in game relationships with many. The multiplayer aspect was also very enjoyable and I spent alot of time playing that. The story (up until the ending) and the characters really where the pinnacle of this game and enjoyed it thoroughly. This entry is at number 9 really because I actualy havn't played it recently comapared to the other entries on the list.




#8 Dragon's Dogma


Dragon's Dogma is a 2012 open world action role-playing hack and slash video game by Capcom. First released for PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360, an enhanced version subtitled Dark Arisen was later released for the same consoles in 2013, with ports releasing for Windows, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and Nintendo Switch in the ensuing years. Dragon's Dogma is on the list because of its amazing world building, and top tier gameplay. Fast paced, impactful, and full of style, no other Open World game at the time came close to Dragon's Dogma's combat. It's story and characters where pretty non existant but in contrast to that, the world's setting and lore of kept an interesting backbone, inticing you to keep going. Really, it's my favorite part about the game, such an immersive experience. If I made a top 10 list back when I played it, i'd definitly rate it higher than the number 8 spot. It's 2024 now and to be honest, the game is outdated. Fortunatly, a sequal came out last year that improves on Dragon's Dogma's amazing combat. Unfortunatly, its story and world building is actually somehow worse than its predecessor and the game was clearly unfinished.




#7 Borderlands 2


Borderlands 2 is a first-person shooter and action role-playing game developed by Gearbox Software and published by 2K Games. Released in 2012, it is the sequel to the original "Borderlands." The game is set in the post-apocalyptic world of Pandora and features a unique cel-shaded art style. Players take on the role of one of four Vault Hunters, each with distinct abilities and skill trees, as they embark on a quest to defeat the antagonist, Handsome Jack, who seeks to control Pandora's resources. The game combines shooting mechanics with RPG elements, allowing players to level up, collect loot, and complete quests either solo or in cooperative multiplayer mode. This game was a big part of my childhood. Lootin, shootin, slaying, it was all just so entertaining. The story was great, the characters where enjoyable, and the weapons and gear where all fun!




#6 Halo 3


"Halo 3" is a first-person shooter developed by Bungie and released for the Xbox 360 in 2007. It is the third installment in the main Halo series and continues the story of the Master Chief, a super-soldier known as a Spartan, as he battles against the Covenant and the Flood. The game features a rich single-player campaign that wraps up the story arc that began in "Halo: Combat Evolved" and continued in "Halo 2." Players navigate through various environments, using a mix of firearms, vehicles, and the Master Chief's advanced abilities to defeat enemies.




#5 Terraria


Terraria is a precedualy generated open world 2d adventure game where you explore, craft, and fight the forces of evil residing within the world. The reason why this game is on the list is because it is just plain fun! It's gameplay loop is simple but effective. Constantly crafting and ugrading gear, killing bosses for rare drops, and slowly building a base of operations, the progrssion is so inticing. The game is also highly moddable, adding other peoples creations extends this games play time by hours. Terraria is a game I can just sit down and play anytime without any care in the world.
